About Kantanka Rentals

Learn about our story, mission, and what drives us.

Our Story

Kantanka Rentals was founded by a group of KNUST students who saw a gap in reliable, affordable transportation on campus. Getting around Kumasi whether for lectures, internships, or weekend trips shouldn't be stressful or expensive.

Inspired by the pioneering spirit of Kantanka Automobile Company . Ghana's first indigenous car manufacturer founded by Apostle Dr. Ing. Kwadwo Safo in 1994, We set out to build a campus car rental service that puts students and staff first. Our fleet proudly features Kantanka vehicles like the Onantefo, K71, and Amoanimaa alongside trusted international brands like Toyota, BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Honda, and Hyundai.

Since our founding, we have served a good number of satisfied customers across campus and continue to grow our fleet to meet demand championing African innovation while offering world-class choice.

Our Mission & Vision

▶ Mission

To provide the KNUST community with safe, affordable, and convenient vehicle rental services that make campus life easier and more connected. We are committed to excellent customer service, transparent pricing, and maintaining a diverse fleet of Ghanaian and international vehicles that students and staff can rely on every day.

▶ Vision

To become the leading campus transportation provider in Ghana, expanding to universities nationwide and setting the standard for student-focused mobility solutions powered by African innovation and global quality.
